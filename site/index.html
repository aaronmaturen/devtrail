Your Base Camp

The dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your career landscape. See your recent contributions, track progress toward goals, and get AI-generated insights about your work patterns. Everything you need to prepare for your next review, all in one place.

Evidence on the Trail

Every PR merged, every ticket closed, every code review completed - DevTrail captures it all. AI automatically categorizes your work against performance criteria, so when review season arrives, you have a comprehensive record of your impact.

Navigate by Goals

Set SMART goals with clear milestones and watch as DevTrail automatically links your daily work to your larger objectives. Track progress visually and never lose sight of what you're working toward.

Sync Your Sources

Connect GitHub and Jira with a few clicks. DevTrail pulls in your PRs, code reviews, and tickets automatically. Configure which repositories and projects to track, then let the system do the work.

AI Trail Guide

Stuck on how to articulate an accomplishment? The AI assistant helps you craft compelling narratives from your raw data. Ask questions about your work history, get suggestions for your self-review, or explore patterns in your contributions.

Build Your Report

When it's time to write your review, the report builder has your back. AI generates draft content based on your evidence, and you refine it with an interactive editor. Export polished documents ready for submission.
