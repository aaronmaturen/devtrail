# Performance Goals for FY24

## 1. Technical Excellence
**Goal:** Improve code quality and technical leadership across the team.

**Key Results:**
- Lead at least 2 technical initiatives that improve code quality or developer experience
- Conduct thorough code reviews for 90% of team PRs
- Reduce technical debt by refactoring at least 3 critical components
- Document best practices for the team's primary tech stack

**Due Date:** End of Q4 FY24

## 2. Testing & Quality
**Goal:** Enhance testing practices and overall quality of deliverables.

**Key Results:**
- Increase test coverage to 80% across critical services
- Implement automated integration testing for core user flows
- Reduce regression bugs by 30% through improved testing strategies
- Create and socialize testing guidelines for the team

**Due Date:** End of Q3 FY24

## 3. Cross-Team Collaboration
**Goal:** Strengthen relationships with other teams and improve cross-functional outcomes.

**Key Results:**
- Participate in at least 2 cross-team initiatives
- Establish regular communication channels with dependent teams
- Collaborate on at least 1 project with the product team to improve user experience
- Share technical knowledge through 2 internal presentations or workshops

**Due Date:** Ongoing throughout FY24
