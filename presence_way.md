# The Presence Way

## Our Mission
**Our mission is to deliver large-scale impact by empowering all who serve children with diverse needs**

Our goal is to service 200,000 referrals and empower 15,000 providers in the 2026-27 school year

## The Presence Way Framework

### Empowered Providers
- **Be the employer of choice for providers**
  - Provide a path for inspiring careers delivering personal growth, impact & connections
  - Maximize time providers spend supporting students
  - Offer a friction-free provider experience

### Innovative Technology
- **Leverage technology to support positive outcomes**
  - Evolve industry-leading teletherapy platform
  - Optimize our capacity management system
  - Advance the field of digital assessment

### Integrated Solutions
- **Design and deliver a seamless experience**
  - Design solutions based on clinical standards for best-in-class telepractice
  - Embed our services into each partner's overall program
  - Define the optimal onsite environment for teletherapy

### Preferred Partner
- **Be the first call for our partners' needs**
  - Provide premium, best-in-class solutions, providers, and hybrid implementation expertise
  - Empower partners to demonstrate the value of teletherapy
  - Enable friction-free hybrid programs

## Our Foundation
Built upon a highly-effective workforce that values **diverse perspectives**, is **representative** of our stakeholders and is informed by **clinical expertise**.
